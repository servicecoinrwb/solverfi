Frequently Asked Questions

For Investors

How is my capital protected?

Your funds are loaned to real-world businesses against specific, verifiable invoices. Our DAO performs off-chain due diligence on every business and every loan to mitigate risk.

What are the fees?

The protocol charges a 20% performance fee on the yield generated by solvers. 80% of the yield is distributed back to you, the investor.

How do I withdraw my funds?

You can withdraw your initial deposit and any accrued yield at any time through the "Withdraw All" button in our secure dashboard app.

For Businesses

What are the requirements to get a loan?

We fund registered businesses with a proven track record. You'll need to submit an application with your company details and the specific invoice you need to finance for our DAO to review.

What is the interest rate on loans?

Interest rates are competitive and are determined on a case-by-case basis during the due diligence process, depending on the risk profile and duration of the loan.

How long does the approval process take?

Our DAO aims to complete the due diligence and approval process within 5-7 business days after you submit your application.
